 How to preview a code Artifact in Claude.ai

 

 Here is how to preview a code artifact in claude.ai
 First click on the "Artifacts" menu item in the left sidebar

 Next click on the "Your artifacts" tab in the top navigation menu

 Click on the code Artifact you want to preview

 Then click on the preview view toggle button in the top toolbar to switch back to preview mode

 Finally review the preview to ensure everything displays and functions correctly
