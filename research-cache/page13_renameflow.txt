 How to rename a chat from the sidebar in Claude.ai

 

 Here is how to rename a chat from the sidebar in claude.ai
 First locate the chat you want to rename in the left sidebar

 Hover and click on the three-dot menu button next to the chat name

 Next select the "Rename" option from the dropdown menu

 Then enter the new name for the chat in the "Rename chat" dialog box

 Finally click on the "Save" button to confirm the chat rename
